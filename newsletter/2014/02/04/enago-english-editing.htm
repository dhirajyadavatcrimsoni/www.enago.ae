Writing a publishable manuscript is a difficult task and requires good planning, hard work, and persistence.
It is inevitable to write the manuscript in English to communicate the research clearly and reach the broader audience through publication.
It is important to know how Journal Editors, the final decision makers on publishing any manuscript, make decisions. They want to publish good quality research that will interest to their readers; hence, they often have to decide between many high-quality manuscripts.
4 major criteria for acceptance of a manuscript:
It describes research that advances the field
It’s carefully prepared and formatted
It uses clear and concise language
It follows ethical standards
Few other mistakes to avoid:
Poor content and research
Improper journal selection*
Poor English*
Poor manuscript development*
Journal guidelines not followed*
* Enago English editing services can help authors overcome the last four major mistakes that lead to manuscript rejection and increase their publication chances.
